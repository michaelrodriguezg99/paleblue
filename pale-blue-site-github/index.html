FAQ

Who do you work with?

Small businesses and mid-size firms in construction, engineering, IT, and professional services pursuing federal work.

Do you write proposals?

We support proposal strategy, compliance, storyboarding, and color team facilitation. If you need writing capacity, we can help structure a plan and coordinate resources.

What’s the typical engagement length?

Most clients start with a readiness call and a 30–90 day sprint to stand up a pipeline and capture plan workflow, then continue with ongoing support.

Can you help with systems and automation?

Yes. We can implement lightweight CRMs, dashboards, and workflows that make capture and delivery more traceable and less manual.
